# SixPrizer Beta Tester Instructions

Thanks for helping test SixPrizer. This beta is for competitive Pokemon TCG players who want to track games, spot patterns, and get better testing signal from their results.

## How to start

1. Open the beta link.
2. Sign up with your email and password.
3. Check your inbox and spam folder for the SixPrizer confirmation email.
4. Confirm your email, then log in.

## Your first setup

1. Create your profile.
2. Create your first deck.
3. Add your first test version.
4. Log your first game.

After that, try to log at least 10 real testing games if you can. The more real games you log, the more useful Dashboard, Matchups, and Review become.

If something feels off while you test, you can also open `Feedback` in the app and save a quick report there.

## What to look at after logging games

- `Dashboard`
  - quick next-step recommendation
- `Matchups`
  - what looks weak or worth retesting
- `Review`
  - what your recent games seem to be saying
- `Decks`
  - version tracking and deck setup

## What feedback to send in WhatsApp

Please report:

- anything that confused you
- anything that felt too slow
- any broken mobile layout
- any button or card that looked clickable but did nothing
- any coaching insight that felt unclear or wrong
- any matchup, deck, or version signal that felt misleading

If possible, include:

- a screenshot
- your device and browser
- the page you were on
- what you expected to happen

## Known limitations

- Review is useful, but the top recommendation may still over-prefer a matchup watchlist over a repeated loss pattern in some bigger datasets.
- Some setup and review screens are still denser than the match logging flow.

## Privacy note

- Do not upload anything you would not want tied to your own testing notes.
- Public profile/report settings can be changed in the app.

## Feedback channel

Send feedback in the WhatsApp beta group for urgent bugs or screenshots, or use the in-app `Feedback` page if you want to save a report directly in SixPrizer.
